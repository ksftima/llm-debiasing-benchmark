Is ankle/arm pressure predictive for cardiovascular mortality in older patients living in nursing homes? longitudinal descriptive study. 2 large nursing homes in Turin, Italy. 418 dependent elderly (83 males, 335 females, mean age 83.7+/-8.5 y, range 55-102) living in the nursing homes. the prevalence of peripheral arterial disease (PAD) was evaluated using a Doppler Ultrasound measurement of AAI (Ankle/Arm blood pressure Index).
