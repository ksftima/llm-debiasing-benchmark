Does screening or surveillance for primary hepatocellular carcinoma with ultrasonography improve the prognosis of patients? The purpose of this paper is to evaluate the efficacy of ultrasonographic screening for primary hepatocellular carcinoma. We used survival time, tumor morphology, and T staging as prognostic outcomes.
